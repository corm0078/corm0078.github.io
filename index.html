Young Jane

The solution was creating Icons that would be recognize based on colour profiles mostly, then the user could select a more specific feeling . The bring colours reflect moods like red for anger or blue for sadness appealing the users psychological aspect. The Eyes were also made to be an important part of the icons by making them bigger and adding eyebrows. These little changes made it easier for the viewer to spot their icons.The solution was creating an art deco poster that would remind the viewer of the time period for the original piece of work and creating interest with this style that leads the eye through the finish piece.

Meet Me

I can never stand still. I must explore and experiment. I am never satisfied with my work. I resent the limitations of my own imagination.

Why I Design

I believe in creating; creating a story, creating an experience! Yes designs should be beautiful but far more then that, design should have a purpose, a meaning , an action! Design is what I do, but the thought behind the design, the communication aspect, and how the user understands the design, is the reason I do it!

Who Am I

I’ m an old lady but sometime I’m a child I never feel the need to act my own age because what fun would that be ? I watch way too many disney movies and quote Winnie the Pooh non-stop. I really enjoy card games, jenga, tea and desperatly want to learn how to knit. I’m way too curiou, I can never wait till my done my coffee to roll up the rim (I always spill it !) I love to focus on the good stuff and try to forget about the bad like dinosaurs (they’re scary). I’m quirky and eccentric but all these thing make me who I am.

UX/Web Design

I really enjoy web and user experience. I first realized the satisfaction of seeing someone use your website. I love trying to understand how the user think and how they will use the final product. When designing the user is always the main person on my mind. If the user is happy and has a understanding of the application then I have done my work. After working on something for so long there is nothing better then showing it to the target market/user to see what they think and how to improove something.
